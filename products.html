ASCON: The NIST Lightweight Cryptography Standard

In 2023, NIST selected the ASCON family as the global standard for lightweight cryptography.

ASCON is optimized for hardware implementations requiring low area, low power, and high security guarantees. It is the ideal solution for IoT, automotive, embedded systems, and any application where resources are constrained but security cannot be compromised.
